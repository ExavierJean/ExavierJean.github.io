ABOUT ME

From amateur skateboarder to a Full-Fledged Web Developer. Ever since I was a young man skating the streets of Florida, I was crazy about ones and zeros and you can make nothing into something on anything with a screen. I didn’t start chasing this curiosity until I moved to Boston and got into a really bad skateboarding accident. Not being able to skateboard until my body was healed made me depressed so I put my energy towards school. My history teacher realized that I’m a sticklers for numbers and found a program called resilient coders. Made an appointment to meet with Del, Founder and CEO of Resilient Coders, and from there history was made.
